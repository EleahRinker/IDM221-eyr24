Churro Cheesecake Bites

Home Breakfast Protein Bowls Desserts Snacks

Ingredients

  • 2 cans crescent roll dough
  • 2 (8 oz) cream cheese blocks, softened
  • 1 cup sugar, divided
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Press one can of dough into the pan.
  3. Mix cream cheese, 1/2 cup sugar, and vanilla; spread over dough.
  4. Top with the second can of dough.
  5. Brush with butter and sprinkle with cinnamon sugar.
  6. Bake for 25–30 minutes and let cool before slicing.
