Campfire Hash

Home Breakfast Protein Bowls Desserts Snacks

Ingredients

  • 2 cups diced potatoes
  • 1 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1 cup plant-based sausage or tofu cubes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Optional: chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add potatoes and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add bell peppers, onion, and plant-based sausage; cook 5–7 more minutes until veggies are tender.
  4. Season with smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.
  5. Garnish with parsley and serve warm.
