Protein Pancake Bowl

Home Breakfast Protein Bowls Desserts Snacks

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 scoop plant-based protein powder
  • 1/2 cup plant-based milk
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Optional toppings: sliced banana, berries, nut butter, seeds

Instructions

  1. Blend oats in a blender until they become a fine flour.
  2. In a bowl, mix oat flour, protein powder, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add plant-based milk and vanilla extract; stir until a smooth batter forms.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease it.
  5. Pour batter to form pancakes of desired size; cook 2–3 minutes on each side until golden.
  6. Stack pancakes in a bowl and add desired toppings like fruit, nut butter, or seeds.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy a protein-packed breakfast!
