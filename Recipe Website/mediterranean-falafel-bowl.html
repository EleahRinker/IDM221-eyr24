Mediterranean Falafel Bowl

Home Breakfast Desserts Snacks Protein Bowls
]

Ingredients

  • 3 falafel patties
  • 1/2 cup cooked couscous
  • 1/4 cup hummus
  • 1/4 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup tomato, diced
  • Tahini drizzle

Instructions

  1. Prepare falafel and couscous according to instructions.
  2. Arrange couscous, falafel, hummus, and vegetables in a bowl.
  3. Drizzle with tahini before serving.
