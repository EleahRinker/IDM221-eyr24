Plant Powered Taco Bowl

Home Breakfast Desserts Snacks Protein Bowls

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brown rice, cooked
  • 1/2 cup black beans
  • 1/2 cup corn
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 avocado, sliced
  • Taco seasoning and lime juice

Instructions

  1. Warm beans and corn in a skillet with taco seasoning.
  2. Layer rice, beans, corn, and tomatoes in a bowl.
  3. Top with avocado and a squeeze of lime juice.
