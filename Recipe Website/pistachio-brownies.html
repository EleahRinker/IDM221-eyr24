Pistachio Crunch Brownies

Home Breakfast Protein Bowls Desserts Snacks

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped pistachios
  • 1/4 cup white chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a square baking pan.
  2. Whisk butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla until smooth.
  3. Add cocoa, flour, and salt, mixing until just combined.
  4. Fold in pistachios and white chocolate if using.
  5. Bake 20–25 minutes until edges are set but center is fudgy.
