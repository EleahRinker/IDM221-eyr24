Sweet & Sour Tofu Bowl

Home Breakfast Protein Bowls Desserts Snacks

Ingredients

  • 1 block firm tofu, pressed and cubed
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks (fresh or canned)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce (or tamari)
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar or maple syrup
  • 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water (slurry)
  • Cooked rice or quinoa, for serving
  • Optional garnish: sesame seeds, sliced green onion

Instructions

  1. Press the tofu for 10–15 minutes to remove excess water, then cut into cubes.
  2. Heat a tablespoon of oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add tofu and cook until golden and crisp on all sides. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add a little more oil if needed and sauté the sliced onion and bell pepper until softened, about 3–4 minutes.
  4. Add the pineapple chunks and cook 1–2 minutes more to warm them through.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, rice vinegar, ketchup, and brown sugar (or maple syrup).
  6. Pour the sauce into the pan and bring to a simmer. Stir in the cornstarch slurry and cook until the sauce thickens slightly.
  7. Return the tofu to the pan and toss to coat everything evenly in the sauce. Cook 1–2 minutes more to heat through.
  8. Serve over cooked rice or quinoa and garnish with sesame seeds and sliced green onion if desired.
