Indian Curry Bowl

Home Breakfast Desserts Snacks Protein Bowls

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice, cooked
  • 1/2 cup chickpeas
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp curry paste
  • 1/4 cup spinach
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Warm curry paste in a saucepan.
  2. Add chickpeas and coconut milk, simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Stir in spinach until wilted.
  4. Serve over rice with a sprinkle of pepper.
