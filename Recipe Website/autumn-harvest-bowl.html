Autumn Harvest Bowl

Home Breakfast Protein Bowls Desserts Snacks

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa, cooked
  • 1 cup roasted sweet potatoes
  • 1/2 cup kale, chopped
  • 1/4 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/4 cup pecans
  • 2 tbsp maple vinaigrette

Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa and let it cool slightly.
  2. Roast sweet potatoes at 400°F for 25 minutes.
  3. Combine quinoa, potatoes, kale, cranberries, and pecans.
  4. Drizzle with maple vinaigrette and toss to coat.
