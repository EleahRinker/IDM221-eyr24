Brown Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies

Home Breakfast Protein Bowls Desserts Snacks

Ingredients

  • 1 cup browned butter, cooled
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Whisk browned butter with sugars until smooth, then add eggs and vanilla.
  3. Stir in flour, baking soda, and salt until a dough forms.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Scoop dough onto the baking sheet and bake 10–12 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Cool on a rack and enjoy warm.
