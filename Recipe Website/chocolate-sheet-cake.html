Easy Chocolate Sheet Cake

Home Breakfast Protein Bowls Desserts Snacks

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup hot water
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a sheet pan.
  2. Whisk flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, and salt together.
  3. Stir in eggs, buttermilk, and vanilla.
  4. Heat butter and water together until melted, then mix into batter.
  5. Pour into pan and bake for 20–25 minutes.
