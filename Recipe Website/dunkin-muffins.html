Dunkin’-Inspired Muffins

Home Breakfast Protein Bowls Desserts Snacks

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup plant-based milk
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Optional: chocolate chips, blueberries, or nuts

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, whisk plant-based milk, oil, and vanilla extract.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed.
  5. Fold in optional mix-ins like chocolate chips or blueberries.
  6. Fill muffin cups 3/4 full and bake for 18–20 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Let cool for 5–10 minutes before serving.
