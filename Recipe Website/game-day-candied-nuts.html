Game Day Candied Nuts

Home Desserts Protein Bowls Breakfast Snacks

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed nuts
  • 1/3 cup maple syrup
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Mix all ingredients in a bowl until nuts are evenly coated.
  3. Spread evenly on baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 15–20 minutes, stirring halfway through.
  5. Cool completely before serving.
