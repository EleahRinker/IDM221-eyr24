Oatmeal Cookie

Home Breakfast Protein Bowls Desserts Snacks

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup oats
  • 1/2 mashed banana
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 scoop protein powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbs melted coconut oil
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Optional: chocolate chips, raisins, or nuts

Instructions

  1. Preheat airfryer to 350°F (175°C) and line it with parchment paper.
  2. Mash banana & mix all the ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Fold in chocolate chips or other mix-ins if desired.
  4. Scoop dough onto the baking sheet and flatten slightly.
  5. Airfry for 10–12 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Let cool for 5 minutes before serving.
