Air Fryer Fries

Home Snacks Protein Bowls Breakfast Desserts

Ingredients

  • 2 large russet potatoes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cut potatoes into thin fries and soak in water for 30 minutes.
  2. Drain, pat dry, and toss with oil and seasonings.
  3. Air fry at 380°F (190°C) for 18–20 minutes, shaking halfway through.
  4. Serve with your favorite dipping sauce.
