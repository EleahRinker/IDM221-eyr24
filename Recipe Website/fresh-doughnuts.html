Fresh Doughnuts

Home Breakfast Protein Bowls Desserts Snacks

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Oil for frying
  • Cinnamon sugar for coating

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
  2. Mix dry ingredients, then add milk, eggs, and butter.
  3. Drop spoonfuls of batter into hot oil and fry until golden.
  4. Remove and coat immediately in cinnamon sugar.
