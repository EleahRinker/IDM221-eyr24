Mayak Eggs & Veggies

Home Desserts Protein Bowls Breakfast Snacks

Ingredients

  • 4 soft-boiled eggs
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 chopped chili (optional)
  • 1/2 cup chopped cucumber and bell peppers

Instructions

  1. Whisk soy sauce, vinegar, sesame oil, sugar, and chili.
  2. Place eggs and veggies in a jar or container.
  3. Pour marinade over them and refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
  4. Serve cold or at room temperature as a protein-packed snack.
